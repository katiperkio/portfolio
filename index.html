About Me

Hi! My name is Kati Perkiö and I am here to tap into my creative side via the vast world of web design. I am currently based in Vantaa, Finland.

I have been studying full-stack development in Business College Helsinki since August 2023.
This portfolio was initially made by me as a part of the studies, but I ended up loving it so much that I decided to keep it as is for what is to come.
